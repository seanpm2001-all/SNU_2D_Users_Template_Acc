SNU developer log Week 44

SNU developer log day 302

I didn't get what I wanted done again today. I was missing out on some medication again, and I was severely out of focus. I had a huge update planned for today, but I was only able to add in a snippet of it.

Today wasn't a very good day for development, I had a lot planned, but I failed on it again. I did some brief documentation changes, a final update to the Acid test pages for now, and just the very minimal start of the SNU Committee system.

I have the medication issue fixed, I will try again tomorrow.

Sean Patrick Myrick on November 28th 2019

SNU developer log day 303

I was having issues with focus and time today, and I was unable to develop the site. No updates were put out today, I wasn't capable again. I am still getting a hold of these issues and correcting them so December doesn't go as badly with development as November.

Sean Patrick Myrick on November 29th 2019

SNU developer log day 304

I was able to put out a small update today. I updated some held off elements of the site, such as the committee section, and the developer notes section, or the secondary documentation.

I really have been struggling trying to pinpoint how the committee system should work, and I have been having lots of time and focus issues this month, making November the worst month for development this year based on daily updates and the many missed days.

I am going to try harder in December, I am still sorting out some issues, and I hope to have a better holiday development period.

Sean Patrick Myrick on November 30th 2019

SNU developer log day 305

I put out a mid-medium effort committee system update today where I added in a constitution, and added many different committee home pages. I am still recovering from my issues that I have come to identify as tension and music issues. I hope that things will improve from here.

I have some plans for new updates, I just didn't have the time today due to other projects, and a massive lack of focus. School starts again tomorrow, and a week or two later, the Holiday break will occur, and I hope things don't fall apart again there.

Sean Patrick Myrick on December 1st 2019

SNU developer log day 306

I got less done today, but I also had less time. I have adjusted back into my scheduling again, and I had a dentist appointment today at 3:30 pm. I am starting to really run out of basic feature functionality to add to the site, the 3.0.6 update might be a lot bigger than the 3.0.5 as it may include heavily improved scripting.

I wasn't able to fully wrap up the update tonight. Some features got skipped.

Sean Patrick Myrick on December 2nd 2019

SNU developer log day 307

My mood is finally coming back! I was able to produce a decent update today, with improvements in several areas. However it is only a mid-medium (between low and medium) quality update. I am going to start working on getting other developers to help with the project soon (hopefully)

Today, I resumed development on the SNU Galaxy, updated the JavaScript script gallery, and improved documentation. I have a lot planned for the upcoming time. I am now keeping my todo list in the update log for convenience.

Sean Patrick Myrick on December 3rd 2019

SNU developer log day 308

I didn't accomplish too much today. I wasn't feeling good again today. I got severely overheated this morning, and also extremely dizzy and nauseated. I had to spend time to recover, and I didn't get much time for site development.

With the time I had today, I began to add in more semantic tags to the site, the ones I am in progress of implementing everywhere are -section- and -footer-

I also did a significant JavaScript update to the update log for preparation on full legacy Firefox (pre-6.0) support, however this is still just a work in progress. The update log has passed 1 megabyte in size today, not the best accomplishment. I don't have the resources to make the SNU Lite fork yet, so this is just going to continue to grow.

I don't know what I will do about the update log in SNU Lite, I want to include it, but at the same time, it has become insanely bloated, gaining over a megabyte of update and script data this year/

Sean Patrick Myrick on December 4th 2019
