SNU developer log Week 42

SNU developer log day 288

In 1 month from today, I will start development on SNU 3.0.6. I have been deciding on special release dates such as January 30th as SNU 3 day, and May 14th to mark the anniversary of my social media phase

I will end support of SNU 3.0.5 on December 15th 2019. These are just foundation builds and I will release them publically when I am ready, which will be years from now.

I have cleared up over 200 Gigabytes of space for this update, and other projects. Also, I am going to Spokane tomorrow. I am not sure how this will affect development yet.

Today I worked on lots of features, however at the end, I ran out of time, and had to quit early. I still did a lot today, just not as much as I wanted.

I am going to Spokane tomorrow. Today was a decent day for development.

Sean Patrick Myrick on November 14th 2019

I worked slightly on the botcoach program today, with brief documentation changes. I didn't do too much again today.

Sean Patrick Myrick on November 15th 2019
