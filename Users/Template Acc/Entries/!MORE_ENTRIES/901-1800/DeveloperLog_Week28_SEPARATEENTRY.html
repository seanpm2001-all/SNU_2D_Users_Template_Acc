SNU developer log Week 28

SNU developer log day 190

I made a small but important update today. It was a mix of documentation and some new features. One of the most important is a development tool that has all the scripts needed to start a new page. I have been having issues creating new pages, as it felt tedious. Now I have an easier resource to use. I can hopefully start up more features with this.

I also created a GemMine search mode that goes and finds content that is deep into the site.

The profanity filter also got another upgrade, but I still haven't made it functional yet. Everything is still a draft.

Other than that, it was just a documentation update. The idea board didn't get updated today, and the translator didn't get updated either, due to what happened yesterday.

Sean Patrick Myrick on August 8th 2019

SNU developer log day 191

I wanted to do better with todays update. I was unable to due to distractions, and other issues.

Today, a fingernail got cut on my right hand, and it made it more painful to type. Unfortunately, I had to suffer with it, it should grow back with time.

I did some research today, I was simply trying to reference the video tag. I normally go to W3schools to do this, but today, I decided to use Wikipedia. I learned the poster tag, which I really wish I would have known of before. I can now experiment with thumbnails

Today, I did put out an improved update, with some script experiments, new features, and upgraded filters. I was also going to start working on the new SNU Internet Museum section (successor to the classic Legends of the Internet section of the site) but I didn't have enough time left.

Overall, today was a pretty good day for development.

Sean Patrick Myrick on August 9th 2019

A lot of improvements were made. I wanted to do more today, but I couldn't. I will continue work on the new features tomorrow.

Today was a much better day with development.
