SNU developer log day 309

I produced an upate double the size of yesterdays today by commit count, some more was done today, but not much. I am slowly spreading the semantic tags -section- -article- and -footer- around the site, as I find them useful.

I am low on ideas for current site development again, I am just going to work on semantic tags, and adding in new ideas to the ideaboard, along with JavaScript improvements.

SNU developer log day 313

I came back to site development today after taking the weekend off. I produced a small medium quality update today. I am going to start focusing on the advertising of SNU soon, as I need to get more developers to help me with the project.

Today I worked on documentation mainly, and I finalized work on the Firefox browser compatibility script for now, the next steps for the script are support for new versions, and redirection fat binary for switching to different compatibility modes.

I made some progress today, the next big version of SNU starts development in 5 days. I am going to have to actually develop the site the day before, the day of, and the day after the 3.0.6 update.

Sean Patrick Myrick on December 9th 2019
