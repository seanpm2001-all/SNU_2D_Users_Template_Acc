SNU developer log Week 51

SNU developer log day 351

Today was a short development day. I worked mainly on documentation, and memorial and holiday pages. I have decided from now on to keep holiday and memorial pages to a single directory, and not a new directory each year, so that they can be continuously worked on.

I also worked on some other projects, such as another privacy article, and the start of a new call system. Later tonight, I went and gathered resources for the MLK day page, but I was unable to update the articles enough to the standard I wanted them at. I have also decided that I will take 2 days off development of the developer log when it reaches day 299, and update the update log instead for 2 days, so that both pages can reach version 300 on the same day.

I was unable to do a lot of todays update due to a lack of internet on my laptop. The Wi-Fi connection for my laptop hasn't been adjusted yet, so I am unable to connect at school. I needed to get other documents ready beforehand.

The SNU Calling center is a new upcoming feature. Right now, all that exists are the yellowe pages, which are empty. I also need to fix the graphics, as white does not look good on yellow.

I have gotten new ideas, and I will continue further rich development tomorrow (rich development = development with high quality updates per commit)

Sean Patrick Myrick on January 16th 2020
