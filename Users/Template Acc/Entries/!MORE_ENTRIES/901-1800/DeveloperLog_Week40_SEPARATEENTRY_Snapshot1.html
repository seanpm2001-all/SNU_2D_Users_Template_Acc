SNU developer log Week 40

SNU developer log day 274

I had a very short development day today, less than 10 minutes of work was done. This is because I am taking a break from full-on site development for a while.

I am going to try and continue development this weekend, but for now, I am taking a break. I am working on another alternate project since October 24th 2019 that I am using as an alternate medium.

Today is Halloween, and I have no way of celebrating it on the site today. So a simple Happy Halloween will suffice.

Sean Patrick Myrick on October 31st 2019

I also didn't do any updates today to save 3 megabytes, and because I didn't have the time or focus.

Sean Patrick Myrick on November 1st 2019

SNU developer log day 276

I am terribly sorry but I just was not able to work on SNU again today. I am having some severe focusing issues, and other issues with scheduling. I am going to try even harder to do something tomorrow, but there isn't much I can do.

I have officially decided to start learning higher level PHP, JS, and XML so that I can upgrade the site further, and keep going. It is going to take me time. I also am going to need to get a team of developers soon, and self-host the site.

Sean Patrick Myrick on November 2nd 2019

SNU developer log day 277

I was able to get back into site development again. I am not going to drop this project, I have come too far with it just to abandon it. I am taking steps to get back into development again, I have had some severe focusing issues for the past 2 days.

Today, I began my research on advanced JavaScript and XML, focusing today on browser detection, AJAX, and Objects

I did some small documentation updates, but these are just small steps to get back into site development. I can't go on hiatus for too long, as every day of hiatus, it becomes harder to come back, no matter how good the product is.

Development should continue normally again this week.

Sean Patrick Myrick on November 3rd 2019

SNU developer log day 278

I had a short development day today. I was at school again today, and I had less time. Lately I have been messing around with notepad++ themes, so updates might look different in some areas due to the color scheme.

I have added in some new repositories for games that I used to play several years ago that could make a nice comeback as Open Source games. I am working on 2 new repositories for 2 games: Tap Pet Hotel, and Touch Pets Dogs 2, both games have been shut down for over 4 years.

Like WacOS, these are repositories that I won't be putting my full effort into, they are just community projects for anyone who would like to contribute. I am also starting to add in GitHub styling and compatibility for SNU programming pages. A change is going to occur soon with this.

Today was a good day for development.

Sean Patrick Myrick on November 4th 2019

SNU developer log day 279

Development is getting better and better each day of the week. I have a lot of things that I am adding in, and the daily commit count keeps rising (from 0 to 14 to 33 in the past 4 days)

I have finally setted on a good notepad++ theme (blackboard) so it is no longer a distraction. I still periodically change it, as I love customization.

Today, I put some work into the sex education module (adding in the course pages, and support for transgender, and male/female homosexual studies) and I also did some work on programming section repositories and documentation.

I had a good day with development. However, over an hour of development time went solely to the WacOS project.

Sean Patrick Myrick on November 5th 2019
