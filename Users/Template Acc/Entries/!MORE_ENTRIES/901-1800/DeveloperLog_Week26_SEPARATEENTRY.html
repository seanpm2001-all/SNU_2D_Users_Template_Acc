SNU developer log day 180

I have finally decided on a new possible development time system. As by the time I get to website development, I am lower on laptop battery, I normally plug it in, and come back later. I have decided to prevent rushed updates that unless a change to my medication is done, I will be updating later in the night.

Since doing this will give me 100% battery, instead of the usual 80% (or with medication: 46%-55%) I will be able to get a lot more done.

Today, I worked on implementing my plans to remove as many red links as possible, and also offering improvements to site settings, and the new documentation from last night.

I also did some updating beforehand, so that I could do more in the late night update, or still produce a finished update if I ran low on time.

Parts of the update were cut out to save time unfortunately, as I had to quit before 9:20 pm (not that late at night)

On a side note, this has really been bugging me with notepad++ and I haven't bothered to change it yet, but the templateaccountblogs.html file seems to always switch encoding back to ANSI when I open the application, and I have to manually set it back to UTF-8 each time.

SNU developer log day 182

Today was a better day for site updates. I managed to have 2 working sessions, afternoon and nighttime. The nighttime session is where I worked on yourspace. Why did I choose to do it at night? Well, I had to do research on how Myspace page creation works again, so that I could get it just right.

I tried to get as much done with my time and battery life. I had a better focus today. I also had a slightly better time management system.

Today, I began to work on the NSFW (Not Safe For Work) parts of SNU. I have recently done research on peoples opinion on these things, and I have adjusted the development to better suit everyone.

I have also learned about a possible attempt to ban autoplay tags and infinite scrolling. I am not sure if I ever plan on adopting infinite scrolling, so I am fine on that, however the autoplay thing is just cruel.

There are a lot of sites that use the autoplay tag. But unfortunately, some sites abuse it. If for some reason they banned the entire tag just off the bad actions on some, then that would be terrible.

I only have 1 page on my site that uses the autoplay tag for a big purpose, which is the classic Legends of the Internet page. It doesn't use it maliciously. All it does is, when you open the page, it will start playing a 12 second intro video, no sound, it just tells you about the page.

The looping might get removed, and the autoplay might as well. I am not sure yet if I should go through with this.

I am also accepting the fact that Minecraft Py Edition updates don't count as making the website update "significant" as I can't work on both projects at once every day.

Sean Patrick Myrick on July 31st 2019
