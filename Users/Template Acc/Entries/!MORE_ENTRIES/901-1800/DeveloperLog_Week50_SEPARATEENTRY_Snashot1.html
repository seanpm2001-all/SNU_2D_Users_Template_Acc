SNU developer log day 344

I produced an update today where I worked on documentation, web templates, and the renovation of older pages. I discovered that there are hundreds of 2D mode pages that aren't up to date, and have lots of potential. The first one I fixed today was the Amish easter egg page.

I am starting to focus on older pages. Today, I also updated the Earth day page from last year, and started an Earth Day page for this year early. I have also decided to archive comments in the update log again, I will try to bring back older comments someday soon.

I worked on the site for several hours today. There are so many page standards that it takes time to make pages.

Sean Patrick Myrick on January 9th 2020

SNU developer log day 346

I had a nice visit to my friend Michaels house last night. We played Arkham horror. It inspired me to make a card player system for SNU. I am writing it in JavaScript, as JavaScript is similar to Java, and that is where I wrote my first digital card game. This will be a new SNU mode, that specifically plays card games. I might get Michael as a voice actor, as he was a lot of fun while we played. I am planning on calling the codename for this project "cardStack". Cardstock was an option, but I liked CardStack better.

Today, I worked on the CardStack system, along with minor documentation improvements. I was set back on my goals, and I might not get everything I want done this weekend unfortunately. There is still 1 day left, so there is still some hope for it.

Sean Patrick Myrick on January 11th 2020
