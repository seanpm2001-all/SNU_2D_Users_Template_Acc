I got to work really late today due to some medication issues, and I didn't have many new ideas for today, so today was the first somewhat normal update in a while. I updated some of the core pages, and that is pretty much it.

I am still getting a lot done. I updated the idea board and update log heavily, and I did a little tweak to the bottom of the developer log (current page) to have more transparent boxes. Site development is still going great
